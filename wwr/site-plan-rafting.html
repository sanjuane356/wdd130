Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website would be to advertise our white water rafting business to tourist who want to try rafting for the first time and locals who want to plan a fun activity. The website will help with the planning and booking of rafting trips, schedule availability, directions, and other necessary information to prepare people for their trip.

Audience

Who are the target customers? The primary audience we want to reach for this website are for people who are visiting from out of state and local groups who are looking for group activities. What do they Want? The website will give visitors information about where we are located at, what type of services we provide with out rafting trips, and what our available times are, and the necessary information to sign up people for a group. What do they expect to find by them accessing the website? all the necessary information to start organizing a trip for either a large group of a small group of friends. scheduels, maps, our staff information, any forms needed, and pricing options and available services that we provide.
